      About Ptotem

      Ptotem is a game development firm that uses gaming design and technology to solve real world business challenges. A pioneer of Serious Games in India, we create games to power your learning, marketing and research initiatives.

      Ptotem was started in 2010 by senior industry professionals from the fields of Business, Instructional Design, HR Management and Psychology. Over the last three years, we have developed and demonstrated expertise along the three key vectors of serious gaming - technology, design and gaming itself. With international board games, design awards and a marquee clientele underpinning our credibility today, we are now gearing up to play the game the way it was meant to be played - big.

      So keep watching this space for more. The game has only just begun…

About Ptotem

These were the yesterdays. It was when our parents would tell us to stop playing those silly computer games. “Go and read your books instead”, they had said. “These computer games are addictive and would never get you anywhere”, they had added. Who would ever have thought that these games would make a multibillion dollar industry. Who would ever have thought of these games becoming a core part various tutelage designs. It was the year 2009. A few senior Corporate Professionals had decided to put end to their mundane corporate existence and lifestyle, and would go on to create Ptotem Learning. The idea would be a simple one – to get together people to play games. Ptotem would build board games and cause to people to be entertained. And then Ptotem would build simulations and cause people to learn.


About Ptotem

Ptotem is a game development firm that uses gaming design and technology to solve real world business challenges. A pioneer of Serious Games in India, we create games to power your learning, marketing and research initiatives.

Ptotem was started in 2010 by senior industry professionals from the fields of Business, Instructional Design, HR Management and Psychology. Over the last three years, we have developed and demonstrated expertise along the three key vectors of serious gaming - technology, design and gaming itself. With international board games, design awards and a marquee clientele underpinning our credibility today, we are now gearing up to play the game the way it was meant to be played - big.

So keep watching this space for more. The game has only just begun…
